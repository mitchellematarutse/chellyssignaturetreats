Our Brief History

Chelly's Signature Treats is a local inclusive bakery based in Cape Town, South Africa that has played a role in serving the community since 2022.
It was founded on a simple love of baking and a desire to share that joy with others. What started as a small kitchen experiment has blossomed into a beloved local bakery,
known for its delicious, moist and sssshandcrafted, made-to-order treats that bring smiles to faces and warmth to hearts.
